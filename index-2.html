IT CODE FAIR

CDU IT code fair began in 2014. Since then, it has been constantly attended by a record number of students, industry partners, and members of the IT community every year.
All CDU IT students are invited to attend. This year there will be a schools competition

AWARDS ANDPRIZES

The Code Fair encourages IT student to showcase their learnings:

  • Recognizing academic excellence; (academic excellence awards)

  • Supporting creative coding development, (coding competitions)

  • Displaying problem solving and 21st century communication skills (business innovation challenge, poster and digital storytelling competitions)

INDUSTRY AND JOBS

IT Students present their innovative ideas to the university and IT business community, meet local businesses with a view to facilitate future employment pathways.
Employer Speed Dating is a lot of fun. Students have even scored themselves a job!
