For Industry

Code Fair For Industry

Since 2014, CDU Information Technology have hosted a Code Fair to celebrate and promote coding. The NT Government through the Department of Corporate Information Services, the NT ICT Industry Association, and the Australian Computing Society have been ongoing supporters of the event.

The event provides an opportunity for the local industry to engage with motivated IT students and further gain insights into the work being done at CDU. Join us in 2017.

Get Involved

Industry Partners can participate in 3 ways:

  • • Sponsor a competition prize

  • • Judge at the Code Fair

Employer Speed Dating

Employer Speed Dating is where students (divided into two categories: coders and networking and support) give a one minute pitch to prospective employers about what they can bring to an IT organisation. Employers then have two minutes to ask students a range of questions before the buzzer goes and the speed dating begins again.
