Events Schools Can Participate In

The CDU code fair is not just for CDU students and industry, school students interested in Information technology can participate as well!

Check out the links below to see how you and your schools can benift for the CDU code fair.

Hour of Code Workshops

The Hour of Code, began as a one-hour introduction to computer science to demystify "code", and has become a global event. Celebrated as part of Computer Science Week, honouring the birthday of Grace Hopper, the week is normally held in early December. At CDU we celebrate coding early November and are available to run workshops for schools from late September onwards.
